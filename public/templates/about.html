The Game

MathMasters is a realtime multiplayer math game. Players join the game, and then a series of rounds will begin. Each rounds presents a math problem and the first to solve it right wins the round. Be careful though! You get 300 points for getting a question right, but -100 points for getting it incorrect. When all the rounds have ended, the highest score wins!
